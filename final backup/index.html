Native American imagery in sports logos
Athletic teams have long appropriated the symbols of Native American cultures in their logos and mascots. But pressure from advocacy groups has caused many teams and universities to either adopt new logos or to seek permission from Native American tribes to make use of their imagery. Presented here is a collection of logos that have been subject to recent scrutiny. On hover, logos with red backgrounds belong to teams or schools that have retained their Native American imagery. Logos with green backgrounds belong to teams or schools that have since changed their iconography or received a tribe's sanction.
