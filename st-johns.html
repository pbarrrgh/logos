St. John's University

St. John's University in New York originally used 'Redmen' as the nickname for its athletic teams. In 1955 the school introduced a cartoonish drawing of a Native American in a headdress dribbling a ball as an alternate logo for its basketball team.

In 1980 the school dropped the image of the Native American, replacing it with a stylized rendering of a more-anonymous basketball-playing figure. The school continued to use the Redmen nickname.

In 1994 the school, under pressure from outside groups, decided to change its nickname as well. The school's athletic teams are now known as the Red Storm.
